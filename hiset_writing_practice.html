Essay prompt

Some people believe students should be required to learn a second language in school. Others believe this requirement is unnecessary.

Write an essay presenting your position on this issue. Use specific reasons and examples to support your argument. You have 45 minutes on the real test.

0 words
Scoring rubric (1–6 per category)
Development
Clear position with well-developed, specific support
Organization
Logical intro, body paragraphs, and conclusion
Language use
Varied sentences, precise word choice
Conventions
Grammar, punctuation, and spelling
Quick tips
  • → State your position clearly in the first paragraph — don't make the reader guess
  • → Each body paragraph = one main reason + a specific example
  • → Use transition words: first, however, for example, therefore, in conclusion
  • → Restate your position in the conclusion — don't introduce new ideas
  • → Leave 3–4 minutes to proofread for run-ons, fragments, and spelling
